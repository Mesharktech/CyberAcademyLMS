Welcome to AfricaHackon

AfricaHackon is Africa’s pioneering cybersecurity collective, dedicated to building a resilient and skilled cybersecurity community through research, capacity building, and knowledge sharing. As the continent’s premier cybersecurity hub, we are committed to shaping the next generation of experts by providing world-class training, mentorship, and opportunities.

Dedicated to cybersecurity excellence, Africahackon drives research, capacity building, and expert collaboration across Africa. By nurturing talent and fostering innovation, we work towards a more secure and resilient continent. We empower individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge needed to combat evolving cyber threats. Through strategic partnerships and continuous learning, we are shaping the future of cybersecurity in Africa.

frequently asked questions

here's our common question we had so far

Below are some of the most common questions we receive about our programs, events, training, and how you can get involved. If you don’t find the answer you’re looking for, feel free to reach out to us! 

What does cybersecurity look like today?

Cyber threats like ransomware, data breaches, and hacking are increasing, affecting businesses, governments, and individuals. As digital risks grow, so does the demand for skilled cybersecurity professionals to protect systems and data

How does Africahackon contribute to cybersecurity?

Africahackon is building Africa’s cybersecurity talent through structured training at our academy (make this into a hyperlink to the academy page), hands-on workshops, hackathons, and mentorship programs. We also offer cybersecurity training through the Africahackon Academy, equipping learners with practical skills in ethical hacking, network security, cyber defense, and compliance to prepare them for real-world challenges. Additionally, we foster collaboration between industry experts, businesses, and aspiring professionals to strengthen Africa’s cybersecurity landscape.

How will I benefit from Africahackon?

• Learn in-demand cybersecurity skills with hands-on training.
• Receive mentorship and network with industry leaders.
• Work on real-world projects to build practical experience.
• Boost your career with certifications and job opportunities.
• Join a growing cybersecurity community in Africa.

Africahackon is your gateway to a thriving cybersecurity career

What will I gain from this program?

This isn’t just about theory—you’ll get hands-on training, real-world experience, and mentorship from industry professionals. By the end, you’ll be able to apply cybersecurity skills confidently and take steps toward a career in the field.

Do I need a tech background to join?

Not at all! We break things down step by step, making sure you learn by doing. If you're new to tech, we'll help you build a strong foundation. If you already have experience, this program will sharpen your skills and give you practical exposure.

How is the program structured?

The academy runs for six months with interactive lessons, mentorship, and hands-on assignments. You’ll work in small squads with a mentor and attend monthly hybrid (online + in-person) sessions for practical learning and networking.

The sessions run twice a week of 2.5 hours each and last saturday of the month is a hybrid session for a full day (9am-4pm)

 

What are the payment terms?

We offer two payment options:

  • Full payment before the deadline that will be communicated via email.
  • Tiered payment in two installments, with specific deadlines communicated via email.
    Since slots are limited, timely payment is required to secure your place. If you miss this round, you’re welcome to apply for the next cohort.
What happens after I complete the academy?

By the time you graduate, you’ll have:

  • Practical cybersecurity skills you can apply immediately
  • Real-world projects to showcase to potential employers
  • Industry connections that open doors
  • A recognized certification + Credly badges to boost your credibility
How else can I get involved with AfricaHackon?

Beyond the academy, we host cybersecurity conferences, hackathons, Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, and networking events. If you’d like to volunteer, mentor, or collaborate, we’d love to have you onboard!
